The Story of The Blood Night

“The Blood Night’ is an event that took place in the Middle Ages.

There has been a virus outbreak changing normal people into deformed undead beings, craving for blood. The Hunters’ guild has sent you, a hunter of supernatural creatures, and tasked you with rooting out this ravaging virus before it spreads any further .

But be aware not to succumb to the very illness you try to eradicate
